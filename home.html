About Me

I am a 2nd year Computer Science student at the University of British Columbia. In my free time, I enjoy going to the gym, boxing, playing games and watching the NBA.

For as long as I can remember, I've always loved building and creating things. Baby blocks became Legos, Legos became Gundam model kits, then Gundam model kits became PCs. I am just so fascinated by how you can take such small things, and if you put them together just the right way, you can end up with something truly great. It's like magic to me.

“The whole is greater than the sum of its parts.”
- Aristotle

And although the first time I heard this quote was in Grade 12, I was already believing it my entire life. My first dream job was to be an architect! My creativity would be able to roam free and I could build to my hearts content. It wasn't until I started playing PC games and building PCs that I realized my love for computers. My brother in-law introduced me to the world of Software Engineering and when I found out that I could express my creative side while building things, I was hooked and never looked back.
